Elements of Life

Elements & Compounds

Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space.

An element is a pure substance that cannot be broken into simpler substances by chemical reactions.

A compound is a combination of elements in a fixed ratio.

Essential elements for an organism are those that are required for health and reproduction within its species. Most life on Earth requires oxygen (O), carbon (C), hydrogen (H), and nitrogen (N), among others.
