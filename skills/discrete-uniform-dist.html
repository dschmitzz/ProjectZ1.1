Discrete Uniform Distribution

Random variable \(X\): \(x_1, x_2, ..., x_n\) all have equal probability within range \(n\).

probability mass function: \(P(X=x_i) = f(x_i) = {1 \over n}\)

Discrete Uniform Mean & Variance

Given a discrete uniform random variable \(X\) for of consecutive integers \(a, a+1, a+2, ..., b\) on range \(a \leq b\)

mean, \(\mu = E(X) = {b + a \over 2}\)

variance, \(\sigma^2\ = V(X) = {(b - a + 1)^2 - 1 \over 12}\)
