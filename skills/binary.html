Bit

A bit is the most basic unit of information in computing.

One bit represents a value of either \(0\) or \(1\).

Binary Numbers

A binary number is represented as a string of bits, also called a bit vector.

While decimal numbers have a \(1\)s, \(10\)s, \(100\)s place, etc., binary numbers have a \(1\)s, \(2\)s, \(4\)s, \(8\)s place, etc.
