Binomial Distribution

Conduct a series of n Bernoulli trials (those resulting in either success or failure, independent of each other, with constant probability p of success, 0 < p < 1).

Random variable X = # of successes after n trials

probability mass function: f(x) = C(n, x)px(1 - p)n-x
x = 0, 1, ..., n

Shaq's free throw percentage p is 0.527 (he makes 52.7% of free throws).

Binomial Mean & Variance

Given a binomial random variable X with probability p of success and n trials:

mean, μ = E(X) = np

variance, σ2 = V(X) = np(1 - p)
