Box Plots

Box Plot

The box plot, or box-and-whisker chart , shows a dataset's center, spread, shape, and outliers (SOCS).

It shows a 5-number summary: min, \(q_1\), median, \(q_3\), and max.

An outlier is a data point more than \(1.5\) interquartile ranges below \(q_1\) or above \(q_3\).

An extreme outlier is more than \(3\) interquartile ranges below \(q_1\) or above \(q_3\).
