Poisson Distribution

Poisson Process

Poisson process: Consists of small intervals Δt. As Δt tends to 0,

1. The probability of more than one event in the subinterval tends to 0.

2. The probability of one event in the subinterval tends to λΔt.

3. Events within subintervals are independent of those in other subintervals.

Poisson Distribution

Random variable X: # of events in a Poisson process

Given 0 < λ and T as the length,

probability mass function: f(x) = e-λT(λT)x/x!

x = 0, 1, 2, ...
